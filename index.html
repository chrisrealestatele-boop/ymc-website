FAQ

Common Questions

What kind of properties does YMC buy?

We buy most types of residential properties — single family houses, townhomes, condos, duplexes, multi-tenant properties, apartments, and even the occasional commercial property. We operate in Northwest Indiana and the South Suburbs of Chicago, focusing on fast, as-is cash offers.

How does the process work when selling with us?

It starts with a free in-home consultation. After reviewing your property, we make a fair, all-cash offer to purchase your house. If you accept, we pay cash, cover all normal closing costs, and handle repairs and showings ourselves — with a closing timeline that's flexible around you.

What does selling "as-is" mean?

We buy houses as-is. That means we look at your house as it stands today and make an offer — you're not responsible for repairs, painting, or clean-up. We handle everything after closing.

Is there any fee for selling my house with YMC?

There is no fee for selling with us. The evaluation and offer process are completely free, with no obligation to accept.

What are the benefits of a cash offer?

The biggest advantages are convenience and simplicity. A cash offer eliminates lender requirements and financing contingencies, so we can move forward even on properties that need significant repairs — and you get your funds quickly.

How is YMC different from a real estate agent?

Unlike an agent who lists your home on the MLS and waits for a buyer, YMC purchases your home directly. You get a guaranteed offer from an actual buyer, instead of waiting and hoping for one to come along.
